FAQ
Frequently asked questions
Is TrackMyGigs free? +
Yes. The free plan lets you track up to 10 gigs with full access to mileage tracking, invoicing, and commission calculations. No card required to start.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. No contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account settings whenever you want.
Does TrackMyGigs calculate mileage automatically? +
Yes. Set your home postcode once, and TrackMyGigs calculates the round-trip distance to every venue using HMRC's approved 45p per mile rate. No manual logging.
How does TrackMyGigs handle agency commission? +
When you log a gig, enter your gross fee and the agency. TrackMyGigs applies the commission rate you've set and shows your actual take-home instantly. Commission is tracked separately in your tax reports as an allowable expense.
Does TrackMyGigs work with Making Tax Digital? +
TrackMyGigs keeps your income, mileage, and expenses organised automatically with quarterly summaries. When MTD quarterly reporting becomes mandatory for your income level, you'll already have everything in order.
Is TrackMyGigs only for singers? +
No. TrackMyGigs works for any self-employed UK performer - singers, session musicians, DJs, pianists, guitarists, tribute acts, wedding bands, function musicians, entertainers. If you do gigs and need to track income and expenses for tax, TrackMyGigs is for you.
Can gigs be sent to me from band leaders or agencies? +
Yes - this is one of TrackMyGigs's unique features. If your band leader uses Musician Tracker or your agency uses ClientFlow, gigs can arrive in your TrackMyGigs app pre-filled with all the details. Accept with one tap.
Can I use TrackMyGigs for my Self Assessment tax return? +
Absolutely. TrackMyGigs generates a tax summary showing total income, mileage allowance, commission, and expenses - broken down exactly how HMRC wants it. Export and hand it to your accountant, or use it to file yourself.
Is my data secure? +
Yes. TrackMyGigs uses encrypted connections and secure cloud storage. Your financial data stays yours - we never share it with third parties.
